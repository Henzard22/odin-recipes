Garlic Bread

Bread is a hearty addition to any meal or soup, but what about the superior Garlic Bread?
Added in the Hearthfire DLC, Garlic Bread is a welcome sight on any dinner table.
It's a simple recipe, and in Skyrim, eating one Garlic Bread can cure all diseases while still
being tasty and fulfilling.

Ingredients

  • 16oz Italian loaf bread
  • 1/2 cup of salted butter (softened)
  • 3 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley (finely chopped)
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
