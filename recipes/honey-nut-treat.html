Honey Nut Treat

Skyrim is full of wonderful sweets and desserts to try, with the Honey Nut Treat being a sweet
treat enjoyed by all of Tamriel's residents. Mostly made up of nuts and oats, it is quite easy to
make with no baking required, just blending in a food processor. Any of the nuts can be
substituted for alternatives.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pitted dates
  • 1/2 cup golden raisins
  • 1 cup sliced almonds
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter (smooth or crunchy)
  • pinch of salt
  • pinch of ground cinnamon
