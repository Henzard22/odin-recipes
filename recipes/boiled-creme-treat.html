Boiled Creme Treat

Although not nearly as iconic as the Sweetroll, the Boiled Creme Treat comes a close second.
They are a signature staple in the expansive world of Skeasybeing sold by most vendors and
found in most homes. It is a dessert puff pastry with a hint of spice, with a creamy custard
filling in the center.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 1/4 cups of warm milk
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of ground cardamom
  • 4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg, beaten with milk for the glaze
  • custard (for the filling)
